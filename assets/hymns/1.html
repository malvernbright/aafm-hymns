1. MTSVENE MWARI SAMASIMBA

Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Mwari samasimba!
Tinokuimbira pa mangwanani ese;
Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Mune ngoni huru.
Mwari vatatu, mumwechete zve.

Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Vatsvene vanodaro!
Vakapfugamira pamberi pako Mwari!
Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Vanokurumbidza,
Hamusanduki dakara rinhi.

Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Hatizokuwoni,
Zvakaipa zvedu zvavanza huso hwenyu!
Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Ndimi Mutsvene chete,
Makarurama makachena zve.

Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Zvose zvakasikwa
Zvinokudza imi, mukuru kune vese!
Mtsvene, Mtsvene, Mtsvene!
Wetsitsi nengoni!
Mwari musiki Baba wavese.
