*106. ISHE TIGADZIRIRE 7.7.7.7.7.7.*


Ishe tigadzirire
Nguva yokuteura,
Tinopfugama mabvi
Ngatifunge kwauri;
Uvenesu Mponisi,
Tipo rufaro rwako.


Toswedra kwauri,
Tinzwise samba rako,
Tinochema savana
Kuna Baba wa vose,
Tiri pamberi pako,
Tine kutya kutsvene.


Tinofunga Mponisi,
Chitenderano chako
Wakati, “Ndinotuma
Mweya Mutsvene wangu”.
Zarura mwoyo yedu,
Kuti timugamuchire.
