110. TIMUTSIRIRE ISHE  

Timutsirire, Ishe,
Titeure kwauri,
Tivirimise, Ishe,
Tifunge kwauri.
Kukudzwa kwako Jesu
Ngakuchitifadza:
No rudo rwako Jesu
Rwupise kwatiri.
	 
Tiregoneta isu
Pa rukudzo rwako;
Tisatonhore isu
Pano rudo rwako;
O Ishe! tibvumise,
Tikudise iwe.
