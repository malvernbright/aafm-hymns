114. WAKATORA CHIPAPATA 

Wakatora Chipapata
Ukachimedura
Ukati torai mudye,
Zvomuviri wangu.

Jesu watora mukombe,
Wati ngamutende,
Ndo chitenderano chitsva,
Pane ropa rangu.
	
Tino tora chipapata,
Totarira iwe;
Totora tidye tose,
Tichisuwa iwe.

Tinotora nomukombe,
Tichivimba newe,
Timwe kwauri tose,
Tichifunga iwe.
