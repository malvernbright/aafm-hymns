115. WAFA WAFA MUPONISI 

Wafa, wafa, Mponisi!
Akafira vakatyora
Nemirau yake;
Wakafira kuponisa,
Wakafira kuvapinza
Kune denga rake.
	 
Kwete, tsitsi hadziperi!
Kwete, ngoni dzichiripo
Nyamba takatadza,
Ishe unotinanisa,
Ishe unotinyadzisa,
Nezva unoita.

Ishe chifungiso chako,
Chokutiripira kwako,
Dzose mhosva dzedu,
Ngachigare nguva dzose,
Tinzwe ngoni dzako, Tenzi,
Pane mwoyo yedu.
