12.Zadzisa chirevo chako

Zadzisa chirevo chako,
Mwari Ishe wechokwadi;
Ose marudzi enyika
Ngaawane roponiso.
	 
Namabvi kuneyi nyika,
Ngaapfugame panewe;
Dzidzamare dzose ndimi
Dzireve hukuru hwako.

Tonga Tonga, Ishe Jesu!
Kuuye newe kufara,
Nezvikanganiso zvedu
Tatdzisa yose nyika.

Tarira nyika yokwedu,
Urege kuipa kwayo,
Urege kutsamwa kwako,
Isingafe mhuri yayo.

Ramba Ishe tisazvidze
Zvidzidziso zvezwi rako;
Ugozotimutsirira
Tinzwe nechokwadi chako.
