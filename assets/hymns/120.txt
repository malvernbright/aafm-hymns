120. Wauya! Wauya! mucheki mukuru

Wauya! Wauya! mucheki mukuru,
Vanhu vachaona iye She wedenga;
Vanogozomutswa vari ku makuva;
Avo vawonekwe vose vakachena.
	
Zvinhu zva tinowona zvose zvichatsva,
Makomo, mapako, zvichanyongodeka
Zuva richadzimwa, mwedzi uchapera,
Vakaipa vachashaya po kuvanda.

Mucheki asvike, ne hondo ihuru,
Hondo inouya ya vanyai vedenga;
Na meso o Mcheki mukuru aone,
Zvinhu zvakavanda, zvinhu zvakaipa.

Vatadzi vanova no kutya kukuru,
Vanogo dedera pa meso o Mcheki;
Vasaka rurama vachazo paradzwa,
Vaiswe mu nzvimbo yo kutambudzika.

Regai zvakaipa, mutye mururame;
Munzwe izwi ra’She, munamate mose,
Zviteurirei, mutsvake ne tsitsi;
Inguva na zvino, huyai mugamchire.
