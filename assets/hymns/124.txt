*124. PAMSORO PAKUDENGA* 

_(ZULU 252) 7.7.7.7._

1.  Pamsoro pakudenga, 
    Ndavakanaka vega;
    Pamsoro pakudenga
    Kuna vatsvene vega.


2.  Anoda kuenda ko, 
    Ngaazviteurire;
    Anozo gadzirirwa,
    No mweya wo hutsvene.


3.  Jesu wakatifira,
    Kupere mhosva yedu;
    Kuti tiitwe vatsvene
    Naiyo mweya yedu.


4.  Kuti kana tavingwa,
    No rufu pano pasi;
    Tirege kuzorambwa,
    Kuenda pa kudenga.
