*125. Pakudenga kuna Baba*


Pakudenga kuna Baba
Kwakanaka kwazvo;
Pakudenga kuna Baba
Kwakafara kwazvo.
	

Hakupindi chakaipa,
Kana chiri chimwe;
Hakupindi zvokurwara,
Ne chirwere chimwe.


Ndavatsvene ivo vose,
Vanogara iko;
Vanokuda vanamate,
Iwe Mwari wavo.


Vakawana musha wavo,
Musha wokugara;
Vakawana nohupenyu,
Husingapfuure.


Ndiri mweni pane nyika,
Ndinosuwa denga;
Ngandipinde newe jesu,
Ndiyende kudenga.
