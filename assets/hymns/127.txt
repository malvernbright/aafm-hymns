*127. Jerusarem, musha wangu*


Jerusarem, musha wangu,
Musha wakanaka!
Kana ndapedza mabasa,
Ndinoenda kwa uri.


Ndichauwona rinhiko,
Uyo musha wangu?
Une dzimba dzakanaka,
Nokudya kutsvene.
	 

Hapasisina kuipa,
Hapana nenhamo
Ndinoramba ndichienda,
Ku musha mutsvene.


Ndingatye here kurwadza?
Ndingatize kufa?
Kwete ndinoenda Canaan,
Nyika yandinoda.


Vaprofita, vadzidzisi,
Vakafira Jesu,
Varipo pamwe na Kristu,
Neni ndichaenda.


Jerusarem musha wangu,
Wa ndinotsvakisa!
Kana ndasvika kwauri,
Ndichafara kwazvo.
