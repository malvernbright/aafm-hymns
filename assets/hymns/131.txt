*131. Amai ne vana*



Amai ne vana
Vauya kuna Jesu,
Vadzidzi vavadzizvira
Vachiti, “ibvai”,
Asi Jesu haabvumi
Wati kuna vakukutu.
“Regai vaduku vauye kwandiri”.


"Ndinovagamuchira,
Ndinogovapfungata,
Ndichava Mufudzi wavo,
Musavarambise;
Ndinoda vaduku vose
Vagare hupenyu neni
Regai vaduku musavarambise”.


Jesu wakapfava,
Wakavaropafadza;
Nyamba zvihuru zvavana
Vasati vamuziva;
Havana matama ake,
Havanzwe kuti wadaro,
“Regai vaduku musavarambise”.


Tinokuteura
Kuti marudzi ose
Asiye vamwari vavo
Vaue kwauri.
Uvavenekere Mwari,
Uve navo nohupenyu,
Uva dzidzise vauye kwauri.
