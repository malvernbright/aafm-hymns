132 NDIROPAFADZE JESU

Ndiropafadze Jesu,
Dzidzisa mwoyo wangu;
Ndoda mwoyo muchena;
Mufudzi, ndichengete.

Ndiri muduku asi
Wakadaidza vose;
Ndipe zvo kudya zvako,
Mufudzi, ndichengete.

Ndiyamure Mponisi;
Handine samba ini;
Ndinotenda kwauri
Mufudzi, ndichengete.;

Handidi kutsaukana,
Ndoda kugara newe;
Pa nzvimbo yakanaka;
Mufudzi, ndichengete.
