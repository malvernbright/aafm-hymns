*134. NDITUNGAMIRE KUNA JESU*
	 

Nnditungamire, nditungamire,
Ndoda kuenda kuna Jesu:
Ndiye Mponisi, ndingamutende,
Ndoda kumuwona, ndiratidze.


Nditungamire, ndiye Mufudzi,
Mwoyo wake wakapfavisa;
Achitidana vana vaduku;
Achindidana mwana wake.


Ndouya Jesu, Mponisi wangu,
Ndipe tsitsi dzako ne ngoni;
Ndinokutenda, ndikugamuchire,
Ndoda kuzvirurama sewe.
