*136. Ishe wo hutsvene*

 

Ishe wo hutsvene,
Chimbonzwa isu;
Zva tinopfugama,
Tiropafadze.
	 

Zva uri Mutsvene,
Ishe wedenga,
Uchiteerera
Pa kunamata.


Tichiri vaduku,
Tashaya simba;
Titungamirire
Pa nzira yako.


Tidzore pa mhosva
Mazuva ose,
Yamura vaduku
Kukunda kwazvo.


Zva tinoshovedzwa
Tiende uko,
Tichazodavira,
“Tauya Jesu”.
