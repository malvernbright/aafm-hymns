*138. Chiriko chikomo kure*


Chiriko chikomo kure
Kunze kwoyo musha,
Pa wakaroverwa Ishe,
Pa wakafira.


Hatizivi isu vanhu
Kurwadza kwakadii;
Wakarembera pamuti
Ne nzira yevanhu.


Wakafa kuti tipone,
Tivevakanaka,
Kuti tiende kudenga,
Tiponeswe naye.
	 

Pakanga pasina mumwe
Wokutinunura,
Ndiye wakava nesimba
Kutipinza denga.


Jesu wakatida isu
Tisati tamuda,
Takafanira kutenda
Nokumuda kwazvo.
