138 . Chiriko chikomo kure
 
Chiriko chikomo kure
Kunze kwoyo musha,
Pa wakaroverwa Ishe,
Pa wakafira.

Hatizivi isu vanhu
Kurwadza kwakadi;
Wakarembera pamuti
Ne nzira yevanhu.

Wakafa kuti tipone,
Tivevakanaka,
Kuti tiende kudenga,
Tiponeswe naye.
	 
Pakanga pasina mumwe
Wo kutinunura,
Ndiye wakava nesimba
Kutipinza denga.

Jesu wakatida isu
Tisati tamuda,
Takafanira kutenda
Nokumuda kwazvo.
