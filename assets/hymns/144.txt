*144. IMBA YAKANAKA, PA PAUNODIWA*


Imba yakanaka, pa paunodiwa,
Shamwari wedu, no Mponisi,
Iwe uri pamsoro pavo pose
Vanouya kuzoishanyira.


Imba yakanaka inavaviri
No mwoyo mumwechengete norudo;
Havana kutsaukaniswa no kufa,
Vagere vamwe kusingapere.


Imba yakanaka inavaduku,
Vakapihwa iwe pahucheche;
Iwe shamwari wavo wo kudenga
Vachengetwa newe sa amai.


Imba yakanaka pa vanokuda,
Vanonamata ne basa rose,
Ose mabasa anenge matsvene,
Anoitirwa iwe Ishe.


Imba yakanaka pa vanhu vose
Vanorega kukanganwa iwe;
Vazere rudo rwunofashaira
Anokuda vose vanodana.
	

Imba yakanaka pa vanorwara
Vanouyiswa kuna iwe,
N’anga yakanakisa Munyaradzi,
Unoporesa zvirwere zvose.


Pa musi uyo basa rikapera
Vachasangana newe kudenga.
Wakabva uko, ndopa wakaenda
Kumusha kwako usingapere.
