146. MUKA MWEYA WANGU, FAMBA
  
Muka mweya wangu, famba,
Enda kunoita basa;
Rasa nongo dzako, muka,
Inguva yokuteura.
	 
Mazuva akapfuura
Nungo dzako dzakanyanya;
Simuka nhasi chenjera
Kana Mwari achiuya.

Taura mazwi akanaka,
Uve nomwoyo chena,
Mwari ano wona zvose
Zvifungo ne znira dzako.

Muka zvimutsire mwoyo,
Imba pamwe nengirosi,
Dzinoramba dzchiimba,
Dzinokurumbidza Mwari.

Rukurumbidzo ngaruve
Kwauri Ishe kudenga;
Wandichengeta husiku
Wandipamhidzira simba.

Ndauya zve kuzopika,
Tive nechitenderano,
Tanda zvakaipa zvangu,
Ndizadze no Mweya wako.

Tungamira musi uno,
Mabasa a ndinoita:
Ne zvifungo na matama,
Ndikunamate na zvose.
	 
Imi mose pane nyika,
Nemiwo muri kudenga,
Kurumbidzai Mwari wenyu,
Mwanakomana no Mweya.
