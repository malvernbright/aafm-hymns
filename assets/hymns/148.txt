148. MANGWANANI ZVINHU ZVOSE  

Mangwanani zvinhu zvose,
Zvinotenda Ishe iwe;
Iwe uno’penyu hose
Nesu takachengwa newe.

Iri zuva rinobuda
Richifamba nzira yaro;
Iri rinotirevera
Rinoti uri’She waro.

Noyu musi wakauya
Wava nokuyedza kwawo;
Uyu nawo unoreva,
Unzi, uri Muiti wawo.

Na matenga akakomba,
Amire ne minamato,
Ayawo anodzidzisa
Achiti, uri Muchengi
	 
Zvose zvinhu zvatarira,
Iwe kose kudya kwazvo;
Zvenge zvinokukumbira
Kwauri Musiki wazvo.

Tinogoti Baba wedu,
Tikutende, tikukudze;
Iwe M’sununguri wedu,
Na iwe Mweya Mutsvene.
