*149. ISHE INZWA MAZWI ANGU*


Ishe inzwa mazwi angu,
Uchiateerera;
Huna nokufunga kwangu,
Ukuzivisise.


Ndinokurumbidza iwe,
Ishe, Mwari wangu;
Rinonwzika mangwanani,
Newe izwi rangu.


Kose ikoko kutadza,
Kunovengwa newe;
Vose vanoda kutadza,
Vonotandwa newe.


Ngindipinnde ini Ishe,
Kune imba yako;
Ndisangane newe uko,
Noko kuda kwako.
	

Ivo vanoimba newe,
Ngava fare vose;
Vashevedze nokufara,
Pane nguva dzose.
