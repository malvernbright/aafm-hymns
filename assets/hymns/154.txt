*154. Ngoni dzaKristu muponisi*


Mwari Baba, uve nesu,
Utichengete maurano,
Uve nesu vana vako,
Parima tivhenekere.
	

Muponisi tiregere
Zvose zvakaipa zvedu;
Tisunungure maurano,
Parima tivenekere.
	

Mweya huya uve nesu,
Tive vatsvene maurano,
Mwoyo yedu musha wako,
Parima tivhenekere.


Mwari vatatu no mumwe,
Chishamiso chakudenga,
Tidzidzise zvinhu zvose,
Parima tivenekere.
