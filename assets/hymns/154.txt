154.Ngoni dzaKristu muponisi

Mwari Baba, uve nesu,
Utichengete maurano,
Uve nesu vana vako,
Parima tivenekere.
	
Muponisi tiregere
Zvose zvakaipa zvedu;
Tisunungure maurano,
Parima tivenekere.
	
Mweya huya uve nesu,
Tive vatsvene maurano,
Mwoyo yedu musha wako,
Parima tivenekere.

Mwari vatatu no mumwe,
Chishamiso chakudenga,
Tidzidzise zvinhu zvose,
Parima tivenekere.
