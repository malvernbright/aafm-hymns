160.Kudza Jehova Mukuru

Tinoirima minda,
Tinodyara mbeu,
Asi dzinokuririswa
Ne simba ra Mwari;
Anozvituma zvose
Zvo kuchenga mbeu,
Imhepo nero zuva,
Mvura yakanaka

KORUS
    Zvose zva tinazvo
    Zvinobva ku Mwari;
    Tenda kuna Ishe wedu
    Titendere rudo.

Ndine musiki bedzi
Wezvo zvinhu zvose
Anoshongedza nyika,
Na maruva ose.
	
Mhepo inomuteerera,
Anodyisa shiri,
Zvikuru nokwatiri
Anotipa kudya

Tinotendera zvose
Zvinotinakira;
Kudyara nokucheka
No hupenyu hwedu.
Gamchira zvatapira,
Tora ya unoda
Mwoyo yedu-yose
