*160. Kudza Jehova Mukuru*


Tinoirima minda,
Tinodyara mbeu,
Asi dzinokuririswa
Ne simba ra Mwari;
Anozvituma zvose
Zvo kuchenga mbeu,
Imhepo nero zuva,
Mvura yakanaka


*_KORUS_*

_Zvose zva tinazvo_
_Zvinobva ku Mwari;_
_Tenda kuna Ishe wedu_
_Titendere rudo._


Ndine Musiki bedzi
Wezvo zvinhu zvose
Anoshongedza nyika,
Na maruva ose.
Mhepo inomuteerera,
Anodyisa shiri,
Zvikuru nokwatiri
Anotipa kudya


Tinotendera zvose
Zvinotinakira;
Kudyara nokucheka
No hupenyu hwedu.
Gamchira zvatapira,
Tora ya unoda
Mwoyo yedu-yose
