162.HUYAI VANHU TIIMBE

Huyai,vanhu,tiimbe
Rwiyo rwedu rwe zhizha:
Zviyo zvose zvachekwa,
Taka kohwa paminda:
Jehova Wakatipa
Zvose zvatinoshaya;
Huyai ku’mba yake,
Ngatimukurumbidze.

Tiri munda wa Mwari,
Anotsvaka kwatiri
Kudya kwakarurama
Kubve pa mwoyo yedu;
Mapumuri ne hura,
Shure kwezvo zvaibva,
Ishe Tinoda kuva
Zviyo zvakanakisa.

Nokuti Mwari wedu
Achauya kucheka,
Achapisa no moto
Hundi yose yo munda;
Achatuma kumunda
Nhume dzake dzedenga
Dzichaisa zvo kudya
Mune zvitura zvake.

Zvino vose vatendi
Vachaimba zvikuru:
Vachiti taponiswa,
Tabva pazvakaipa;
Tatsveneswa na Jesu,
Tinogara na Mwari,
Imbai imi ngirozi,
Imbai rwiyo rwe zhizha.
Imbai rwiyo rwe zhizha.
