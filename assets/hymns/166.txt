*166. MAPUTE ANOPFUURA*


Mapute apfuura ako
Pamsoro pamakomo
Pa rwizi no tukova,
Chirevo cha Ishe wedu
Tinochirangarira,
Tichazivana zvikuru
Mapute apfuura.
	

*_KORUS_*

_Isu tozozviziva,_
_Hatichazova toga,_
_Pa kuyedza kwa mangwana,_
_Zuva rakanakisa_
_Tichazivana zvikuru._


Tinotsika pa nzira
No mwoyo yaremerwa,
Tinogara mumimvuri
Ohinhambgwe panomumwe;
Asi rudo rwo Mponisi
Rwunodzorera basa,
Kana tosangana uko
Mapute apfuura.


Tichauya no rufaro,
Tinozokomba Jesu,
Pedyo pavo vanotida
Tichazivana ’esu.
No rwiyo rwo ruponiso
Ri charira narinhi
Kana yopera mimvuri
Mapute apfuura.
