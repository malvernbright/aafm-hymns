*169. BABA ZVINO TINOKUDZA*


Baba zvino tinokudza
Imi nehumambo hwenyu,
Muri Dombo re Makore,
Munogara nekugara;
Ruponiso rwenyu Baba,
Runogara rwakadaro,
Vanhu venyu vose Baba,
Vanogara nemasimba.
	 

Honai ruponiso rwenyu,
Runopararira kwose,
Rwunezvose zvakanaka
Ruvimbiso rwohupenyu;
Hatichazotambudzika,
Hatichazoshayi chinhu,
Tina Baba vakanaka,
Muchengeti Mumiriri.


Tinokomborerwa naye
Masikati nehusiku,
Nguva dzose ari padyo
Kuratidza nzira yedi;
Nhasi, tinomukumbira
Mazwi ake akanaka,
Tinokurumbidza iye
Uyo ane simba rose.
