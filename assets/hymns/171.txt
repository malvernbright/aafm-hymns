171 NDITUNGAMIRIRE BABA

Tune: Lead me Saviour
Nditungamirire Baba,
Nditungamirire kwazvo,
Ndinofara ndiri wenyu,
Ndinokudai nguva dzose.
	
Korus
Baba, Baba
Nditungamirirei nhasi,
Kuti ndisasiye nzira
Nditungamirirei Baba.

Muri bako rakanaka
Mumatambudziko ese,
Ndacgengetwa ndiri wenyu,
Ndovimba nerudo rwenyu.

Ishe nditungamireyi,
Misi yose yohupenyu,
Kune nyika yekudenga,
Kune musha wakanaka.
