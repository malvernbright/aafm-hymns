*172. NDIRI MUNHU WENYU, JESU*

_Tune: Let the Lower Light be burning._



Ndiri munhu wenyu, Jesu,
Makandipa ruponiso,
Ndakasiya zvakaipa,
Kuti nditevere imi.


*_Korus_*

_Ndiri munhu wenyu, Jesu,_
_Makandipa simba renyu,_
_Makandiitira tsitsi,_
_Mambo wangu, Ishe Jesu._


Ndinogara nemi, Jesu,
Ndisimbiswe nemi Ishe,
Munesimba kuponesa,
Mungaita moyo muchena.


Ndinotenda, Mambo Jesu,
Mazwi enyu ose ose,
Ndinoendako kudenga,
Musha wenyu, Mambo Jesu.
