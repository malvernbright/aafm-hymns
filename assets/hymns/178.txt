*178. NDI JEHOVA WAVA MUTEMBERI YAKE*


Ndi Jehova wava Mutemberi yake,
Wava Mutemberi yake yakachena.
Nyika yose (Bass)
Nyika yose (All)
Inyarare (Bass)
Inyarare mberi kwake (All)
Nyika yose inyarare mberi kwake.
Inyarare mberi kwake.
Mwari Baba, Tagarira Shoko rako,
Tamirira tinyerere mberi kwako.
