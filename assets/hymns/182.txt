*182. WAGADZIRA NHASI HERE?*

_Tune: have you Any Room for Jesus_


Wagadzira nhasi here,
Nzvimbo yake Muponisi,
Ye kugara mweya wake?
Wakakumirira zvino.
	

*_Korus_*

_Hongu, ndine zvimbo yake,_
_Ishe wa Madzishe ose,_
_Mwoyo wangu ndazarura,_
_Kugamuchira Jesu Kristu._


Mwari wakatisikira,
Kuva imba ye kugara,
Anotuma Mweya Mtsvene,
Kuti azogara nesu.


Une nzvimbo yake here,
Uyo wakakununura?
Apo wanga wakaraswa,
Wakauya kukutsvaka.


Haunacho chipo, here?
Chaungape Ishe wako?
Asi anofadzwa kwazvo,
Kugamuchira mwoyo wako.


Kana uchimukanganwa,
Ungazoponeswa sei,
Zvo kusina rimwe zita,
Raungaponeswe naro.
