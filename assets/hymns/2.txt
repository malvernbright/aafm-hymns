*2. KUDZAI MAMBO*

Kudzai Mambo, anohutvsene;
Arikudenga, anamasimba: 
Ndiye mwoyo yedu ya karekare, 
Chigaro chake chakakurumbira. 


Imbai masimba, ne ngoni dzake,
Arikudenga ne nguo chena;
Hasha dzake dzose dzinotinhira,
Anofamba zvake pachamupupuri,


Anamasimba, wakasika 
Nyika ne zvinhu zvose zvirimo; 
Wakaitsigira no murairo,
Wakaikombera nemvura yose.


Kufudza kwako kusingarebgwi;
Kuri pa mhepo na pa kupenya,
Kuri pa makomo na pa makanga,
Kuri pane mvura na pane dova.


Isu vaduku hatine simba,
Tinotarira chete kwauri;
Tsitsi dzako dzose dzisingapere,
Hama yakanaka no mununuri.


Unamasimba no rudo rwose;
Dzinoimbira dzose kudenga; 
Nesuwo ve nyika tinoimbira,
Tinokurumbidza kusingapere.
