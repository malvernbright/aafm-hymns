34. Mudiwo mweya wangu.

Mudi wo Mweya wangu
Ndinotizira kwauri,
Mhepo inovuvuta,
Ndiri pachamupupuri:
Ndivanze Moponisi!
Pana madutu ose;
Nomusi wokupera
Gamchira Mweya wangu.

Handina gomo rimwe,
Ndokuchemera iwe;
Urege kundisiya,
Ndiyamure netsitsi.
Ndinotenda kwauri
Ndinoponeswa newe;
Fukidza msoro wangu
Na mapapiro ako.

Handidi chimwe chinhu,
Ndinoda iwe woga!
Unomutsa vawire,
Unorapa varwere.
Iwe wakarurama,
Ndiri mutadzi kwazvo;
Ndizere zvakaipa
Iwe wakarurama.

Unengoni gzizhinji,
Dzo kukunda kuipa;
Zarura tsime rako,
Chenesa moyo wangu.
Tsime rakanakisa,
Ndinoda kunwa iro,
Bvira pa moyo wangu,
Gara kusingapere.
