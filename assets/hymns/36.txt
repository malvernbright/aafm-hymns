*36. TINE CHIPO CHAKAKOMBA*


Tinechipo chakakomba
Chatakapihwa iyesu;
Tachipihwa na Jehova,
Icho chipo chainacho.


Chakakomba ichi chipo,
Kwavari vakachiwana;
Vanochida kwazvo icho,
Pamsoro pezvinhu zvose.


Chinesimba icho chipo,
Kwavari vakachiwana;
Cho hupenyu icho chipo,
Kwavari vakagamchira.


Icho chipo ndiye Jesu,
Cha takapihwa iyesu;
Uri chipo chakakomba,
Kwavari vanomutenda.


Tinomutenda Jehova,
Naicho chipo chikuru;
Tinomutenda Mwari woga,
Nokutipa mwana wake.
