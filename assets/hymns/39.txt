*39. MUFUDZI WAVANHU VOSE*


Mufudzi wa vanhu vose,
Rufaro rwo moyo wangu,
Ndoda kuwadzana kwako
Ndinoda kugara newe:
Ndotsvaka mafuro ako
Dopa vanodya vatendi,
Vachizorora kwa uri
Vasinga piswe ne zuva.


Iyo nzvimbo ndiratidze,
Pavanogara vatendi,
Pa vanomutarisisa,
Jesu wakavaroverwa;
Nditaurire rudo rwose,
Kurwadzwa kwako nokufa,
Ndiyende kuneyo nzvimbo,
Ndirobwe ndikunde newe.


Ipapo navanhu vako,
Ndinoda kugara newe,
Kurinda pasi padombo,
Zvimwe pachipfuva chako;
Ipapo ndiregerere,
Handidi kubva kwauri,
Ndichengete Mwari wangu
Pakati pomwoyo wako,
