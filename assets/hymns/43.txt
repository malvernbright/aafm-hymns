43.NDINOKUDA 

Jesu ndinokuda muponisi wangu,
Ndasiya zvose zvakaipa zvangu,
Iwe M’sununguri, iwe M’chengeti,
Ndinokuda Jesu, ndinokuda Jesu,
Ndinokuda jesu, ndokuda zvino.

Ndokuda nokuti Iwe wakandida,
Wakandiroverwa pamuchinjikwa,
Ndokuda nokuti wakandifira,
Ndinokuda Jesu, ndinokuda Jesu,
Ndinokuda jesu, ndokuda zvino.

Ndinogokuda ndichiri mupenyu,
Ndichakurumbidza mazuva ose,
No musi wokufa ndinogoimba,
Ndinokuda Jesu, ndinokuda Jesu,
Ndinokuda jesu, ndokuda zvino.

Kakare, kana ndiriko kudenga,
Ndichagara newe ndichakukudza,
Ndichaimba kwazvo, na vanhu vose,
Ndinokuda Jesu, ndinokuda Jesu,
Ndinokuda jesu, ndokuda zvino.
