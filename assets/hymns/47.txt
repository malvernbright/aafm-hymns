47. MUSI WESABATA


Musi we Sabata,
Musi wakakura,
Wakbuda pane guva
Ishe wokudenga.


Kwakakundwa kufa,
Ratorerwa Guva,
Wakamuka muponesi:
Revai mashoko.
	 

Farai vatendi,
Imbai ngirosi,
Wakakoneswa satana,
Wakagara ishe.


Honayi vatsvene!
Iro guva ra’She
Rigere roga, wamuka
Wakenda Ishe.
