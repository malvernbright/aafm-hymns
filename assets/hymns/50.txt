50.NDINOBVA KWAMURI

Ndinobva kwamuri, ndinoenda kudenga,
Ndinoenda kuimba ya baba;
Kuneiyi imba inohutsvene ‘kuru,
Ndinokugadzirirai nzvimbo.

Kana ndichienda, ndokudzokerai,
A’vanhu venyika vaone;
Kana ndichidzoka ndikugamchirei,Tigare panzvimbo ndinemi.

Ir’zwi ra Mambo, ririizwi rorufadzo
Farai vatendi, farai!
Ishe wakenda, asi achdzoka,
Apinze kudenga imi mose.

Ipapo togara pa hutsvene kwazvo,
Navazhinji zhinji chokwadi;
Ne redu iri izwi tiri pana vamwe,
Tokudza Ishe wekudenga,
