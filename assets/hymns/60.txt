*60. TADAIDZIRWA HUTSVENE*


Tadaidzirwa hutsvene,
Isu vanhu tese;
Paridzai dama rake,
Pane nyika yose.


Kwanzi ngatibude tese,
Pane vakaipa;
Kwanza, ngatirege zvose,
Zvo kupenga kwedu.


Kupenga kwapano pasi,
Kwakatinyengedza;
Icho che chokwadi hechi,
Charurama kare.


Hedzi ngoni noruregerero,
Nedzo ko kudenga;
Urwu rudo no rufaro,
Ndo rwapa hupenyu.
