60.TADAIDZIRWA HUTSVENE

Tadaidzirwa hutsvene,
Isu vanhu tese;
Paridzai dama rake,
Pane nyika yose.
	
Kwanzi ngatibude tese,
Pane vakaipa;
Kwanza, ngatirege zvose,
Zvo kupenga kwedu.

Kupenga kwapano pasi,
Kwakatinyengedza;
Icho che chkwadi hechi,
Charurama kare.

Hedzi ngoni no’regero,
Nedzo ko kudenga;
Urwu rudo no rufaro,
Ndo rwapa hupenyu.
