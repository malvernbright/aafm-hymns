61. JESU ANOGUGUDZA 

Gugu, gugu, ndiyani,
Anogugudza kwazvo?
Ndiye mweni ndishe wako
Wazoshanya kwauri
Ndimashura nawo rudo
Muzarurire m’suo.

Inzwai, inzwai uripo!
Wagugudza nesimb a!
Kana gonhi richiramba,
Ari kurwir’ kupinda;
Uchimira ndimashura
Muzarurire gonhi.

Kana ukanonoka,
Uchiripo nazvino.
Hona ropa namaronda!
Wakabairwa iwe:
Nadiye Jesu Muponisi,
Muzarurire gonhi.
