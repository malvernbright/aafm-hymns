*61. JESU ANOGUGUDZA*


Gugu, gugu, ndiyani,
Anogugudza kwazvo?
Ndiye mweni ndishe wako
Wazoshanya kwauri
Ndimashura nawo rudo
Muzarurire musuo.


Inzwai, inzwai uripo!
Wagugudza nesimb a!
Kana gonhi richiramba,
Ari kurwira kupinda;
Uchimira ndimashura
Muzarurire gonhi.


Kana ukanonoka,
Uchiripo nazvino.
Hona ropa namaronda!
Wakabairwa iwe:
Nadiye Jesu Muponisi,
Muzarurire gonhi.
