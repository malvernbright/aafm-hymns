*63. RIRIPO TSIME RIZERE*


Riripo tsime rizere,
Neropa ra Jesu:
Vatadzi vakashambwa’mo,
Vakachena kwazvo.


*_Korus_*

_Ndinotenda, ndinofara,_
_Jesu wandifira;_
_Na pa muchunjikwa ropa,_
_Randaparadzirwa._


Uyo m’pati wakafara,
Kuwona tsime’ro;
Neni mutadzi mukuru,
Ndinotenda kwazvo.


Iwe Gwayana ra Mwari,
Ropa rine samba;
Kutisunungura isu,
Panezvakaipa.


Handifanire kupinda,
Kudenga kutsvene;
Asi iwe wakaripa,
Mhosva dzangu dzose;


Ndachaimba nero shoko,
Nguva dzangu dzose;
Nokudenga ndichafara,
No kusingapere.
