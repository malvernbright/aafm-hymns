*64. PAIMBA YEGWAYANA RAMWARI*


Pa imba yeGwayana ra Mwari,
Ichiripo nzvimbo yo kupenya,
Pindai, pindai, nzvimbo ichiripo.


Kuripo kufara no kuimba,
Ruripo rudo rwose ne ngoni,
Pindai, pindai, nzvimbo ichiripo.


Tinodaidzwa naye Muridzi,
Anoda kuti vose vapinde.
Pindai, pindai, nzvimbo ichiripo.


Wapera musi ravira zuva,
Kwaiva nemimvuri mirefu.
Pindai, pindai, nzvimbo ichiripo.
	 

Imba yesvitsa yoda kuzara,
Uchakazarurirwa Mukwasha.
Pindai, pindai, nzvimbo ichiripo.


Chenjera kwazarurirwa gonhi,
Kana zvichinzi, “Nzvimbo haipo”
“Haipo, haipo, inzvimbo haipo”.
