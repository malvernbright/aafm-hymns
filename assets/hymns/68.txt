68 .NDINOCHEMA PAMWOYO

Ndinochema pamwoyo,
Ndinochema kwauri,
Ndinoda kuponeswa
Naropa rako Jesu,
Ndinoda kudzokera
Kwauri Ishe wangu.

Ndinochema kudzinzwa
Tsitsi pamwoyo wangu.
Dzokunditungamira.
Pakudzokera kwangu.
Ndifambe nayo nzira,
Igume pakufara.
	
Ndinochema kutenda,
Nesimba rangu rese;
Panewe muafiri wangu
Ndigare ndinofunga
Ndiwe mufiri wangu,
Wandiripira mhosva.

Ndinochema masimba
Anobva kuna Ishe;
Ndinoramba zvenyika,
Ndichienda kudenga.
Ndimutse Mwari wangu,
Ndibude pakipa.
