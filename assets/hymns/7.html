7. MUKUDZEYI IYE MWARI

Mukudzei iye Mwari,
Na vari kudenga;
Mukudzei iye Mwari,
Navari pa Nyika.

Imi muri kokudenga,
Mukudzei iye Mwari,
Nemi muri hondo dzake,
Mukudzei iye.

Iwe, zuva, newe mwedzi,
Mukudzei iye;
Nemiwo imi nyenyedzi
Mukudzei iye.

Iwe moto, newe chando,
Mukudzei iye;
Iwe chando newe bute,
Mukudzei iye.

Ne Mhepo inovuvuta,
Chimukadza iye;
Newe ita izwi rake,
Pa unozonzwiwa.

Imi muri vashe mese,
Mukudzei iye;
Nemi imi vanhu mese,
Mukudzei iye.

Imi mese vakomana,
Mukudzei iye;
Nemi imi vasikana,
Mukudzei iye.

Nemi vanhu vachembera,
Mukudzei iye;
Nemi imi mhuri yose,
Mukudzei iye.
