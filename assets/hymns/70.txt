70.RAKANAKA IRO ZUVA

Ndinochema Ishe Mwari!
Baba uri Mtsvene ‘ndinzwe;
Handino kufara Mambo,
Ndiponese Ishe wangu.

Ndiri hwai, ndarasika;
Nganditsvakwe Ishe wangu;
Ndiri pasi pamapako,
Chindironda Ishe wangu.

Ndakarasa nzira yako,
Ndikarega tsitsi dzako,
Ndikakwira pamikwidza,
Ndikanyura pakaipa.

Pane rima rakakomba,
Buda, zuva rakanaka!
Ngandi pinde pane nzira
Ndiponese, Ishe, Jesu.
