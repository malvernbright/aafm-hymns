*75. KWAENDEPI ISHE JESU*


Kwaendepi Ishe Jesu
Kufariswa kwangu?
Iko kwandaiva nako,
Pakutanga kwangu.


Kufadziswa kwakadaro,
Pano mwoyo wangu;
Nyamba zvino kwakaenda
Kufadziswa kwangu.


Iko kurasika Jesu,
Kwakandinyadzisa;
Pamhidza kundigamuchira,
Zvino ndinopfidza.
	

Newe Mweya wohutsvene,
Pamhidza kuuya
Ndinovenga zvakaipa
Zvakandiparadza.


Ndiyamure Ishe wangu,
Ndizvirase izvo;
Vadzimu vakandirasa,
Ndarasika navo.


Ndive nokufamba newe,
Pakuchena kwako;
Kuti ndikwanise yose,
Mirau yako.
