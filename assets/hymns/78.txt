*78. ISHE WANGU WAINDIDISA*


Ishe wangu! waindisisisa
Pakurasika kwangu,
Ishe wangu wandironda,
Chete mugwara rangu,
Ndawanikwa, Ishe wangu.


Ishe wangu, Iro izwi rako
Ndiwoyo mwenje wangu.
Ishe, wangu, nengoni dzako
Dzinondifarisawo,
Ndinofara, Ishe wangu!.


Ishe wangu, noMweya wako
Undiite Mutsvene,
Ishe wangu, kudenga rako
Iro ndimusha wangu
Ngandipinde Ishe wangu.
