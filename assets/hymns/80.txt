80.TIRI VAWADZANI TIRI VAWADZANI

Tiri vawadzani, tiri vawadzani,
Mhuri ya Jesu Mambo wedu:
Dai tichidana, dai tichidana,
Hapana husvinu kudenga.
	 
Tino yamurana, tinoyamurana,
Vatendi va Ishe mukuru:
Tisingatsaukane, tisingatsukane,
Hakuno kudaro kudenga.

Tiri hondo, imwe, tiri hondo imwe,
Mbirimi dza Mambo mukuru:
Ngatimurwirei nemwoyo mumwewo,
Panzira inoenda kudenga.

Ikoko kudenga kuno rufaro bedzi,
Titende kuna’she mukuru,
Naiwo masimba anobva kuna’she
Tinosvika nawoo kwaari.
