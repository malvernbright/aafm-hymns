82 .FAMBAI VATENDI,FAMBAI KURWA

Fambai vatendi, fambai kurwa,
Munotungamirwa nomuchinjikwa,
Kristu Ishe wedu, mutungamiriri,
Fambai sehondo ne samba rake.
Fambai vatendi, fambai kurwa,
Munotungamirwa nomuchinjikwa.

Satana achatiza, anotya kwazvo,
Fambai vatendi, munogomukunda,
Anozunguzika namazwi enyu,
Simudzira mazwi, imbai mose.
	
Inenda sehondo kokorodzano,
Ichifamba shure vatsvene vose,
Tisatsauraniswa, muviri mumwe,
Mumwe norutendo, mumwe no rudo.

Hunungo paradzwa humambo hwose
Govero ra Jesu rinogosara,
Hondo ya Satana isingagone,
Matama a Jesu achazadziswa.

Ndizvozvo fambai, fambai nesu,
Rwiyo rwokukunda ngatiwombei;
Ngatikurumbidze kwa Kristu Ishe
Nama zuva ose asinga peri.
