83 TIRI PFUMO RA SHE 

Tiri pfumo ra’She,
Tinoenda kunorwa,
Zvino tasimuka kurwa,
Hondo ya’She Kristu.
Tiri pakarema,
Asi tichafara,
Moyo yedu yasimbiswa
Nomutungamiri.

Korus
Tiri pfumo ra’She
Tinomurumbidza,
Tichamunamata iye,
Mambo wava tsvene.

Tiri pfumo ra’She
Wakakumbira,
Fara ne sita rake,
Wakamboroverwa
Pamuti munyoro:
Zvino richiripo,
Remeka zita raJesu,
Nokuti ndi Mambo.

Tiri pfumo ra’She
Tichava na Ishe:
Kana tichitambudzika
Naye Ishe wedu.
Simudza mureza
Nguva yachimbidza
Musi woku pedzisira
Kwe Pfumo ra Ishe.
