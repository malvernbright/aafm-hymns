*83. TIRI PFUMO RA SHE*


Tiri pfumo raShe,
Tinoenda kunorwa,
Zvino tasimuka kurwa,
Hondo yaShe Kristu.
Tiri pakarema,
Asi tichafara,
Moyo yedu yasimbiswa
Nomutungamiri.


*_Korus_*

_Tiri pfumo raShe_
_Tinomurumbidza,_
_Tichamunamata iye,_
_Mambo wava tsvene._


Tiri pfumo raShe
Wakakumbira,
Fara ne sita rake,
Wakamboroverwa
Pamuti munyoro:
Zvino richiripo,
Remeka zita raJesu,
Nokuti ndi Mambo.


Tiri pfumo raShe
Tichava na Ishe:
Kana tichitambudzika
Naye Ishe wedu.
Simudza mureza
Nguva yachimbidza
Musi woku pedzisira
Kwe Pfumo ra Ishe.
