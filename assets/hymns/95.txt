95. NDITARIRE NOKUFARA

Nditarire nokufara,
Ishe wangu!
Ndiwe wanga wandifira,
Ishe wangu!
Waburuka denga rako,
Uchisiya ‘penyu hwako,
Ndanzwa ruponiso rwako.
Ishe wangu!

Ndandigere pae rima,
Ishe wangu!
Ndadnoenda pane rufu,
Ishe wangu!
Dzakuya nhume dzako,
Dzandinzwisa dama rako,
Dzandiudza tsitsi dzako,
Ishe wangu!

Ngandidzinzwe ngoni dzako,
Ishe wangu!
Ndive muponiswa wako,
Ishe wangu!
Zvakaipa ngazvipere,
Mwoyo uve no hutsvene,
Ndinzwe undireverere,
Ishe wangu!
