96. TIMUTSIRE ISHE

Timutsire Ishe,
Huya kuvatsvene,
Taura namasimba ako,
Vanhu vako vanzwe.

Korus
Timutsire Ishe, kana titeura,
Buruka, Ishe, buruka, utikomborere.

Timutsire Ishe,
Mutsa zita rako,
Ngarwubvire rudo rwako,
Pane mwoyo yose.

Timutsire Ishe,
Utiropafadze,
Ngarinzwiwe dama rako,
Rakanaka kwazvo.

Timutsire Ishe,
Tidirire mweya,
Ngakuve hutsvene newe,
Utiropafadze.
