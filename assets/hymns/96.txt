*96. TIMUTSIRE ISHE*


Timutsire Ishe,
Huya kuvatsvene,
Taura namasimba ako,
Vanhu vako vanzwe.


*_Korus_*

_Timutsire Ishe, kana titeura,_
_Buruka, Ishe, buruka, utikomborere._


Timutsire Ishe,
Mutsa zita rako,
Ngarwubvire rudo rwako,
Pane mwoyo yose.


Timutsire Ishe,
Utiropafadze,
Ngarinzwiwe dama rako,
Rakanaka kwazvo.


Timutsire Ishe,
Tidirire mweya,
Ngakuve hutsvene newe,
Utiropafadze.
