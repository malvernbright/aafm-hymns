124 Pamsoro pakudenga

Pamsoro pakudenga
Ndavakanaka vega,
Pamsoro pakudenga
Kuna vatsvene vega.

Anoda kuendako
Ngaazviteurire;
Anozogadzirirwa
No Mweya wo hutsvene.

Jesu wakatifira
Kupere mhosva yedu,
Kuti tiitwe vatsvene,
Naiyo mweya yedu.

Kuti kana tavingwa
No rufu pano pasi,
Tirege kuzorambwa
Kuenda pa kudenga
